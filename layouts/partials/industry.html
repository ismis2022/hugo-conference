We invite you to submit papers to the ISMIS’2020 Industry Session. The session is part of the ISMIS 2020 conference, which will be organized as an online event on Sep 23th – 25th, 2020. SCOPE AND OBJECTIVES The session will be devoted to the applications of various intelligent methods, algorithms, and tools related to Artificial Intelligence in industry. The aim of this session is to create a forum for exchange of ideas between the leading researchers and industry practitioners. Submissions should address practical problems and/or business applications and should be innovative. Architectures, tools, and practical implementations related to the topics described below are also of interest to this session. TOPICS OF INTEREST Topics of interest include (but are not limited to) the following:

  • Applications of Intelligent Information Systems
  • Big Data
  • Cloud Computing
  • Context-aware Computing
  • Exploratory Data Analysis
  • Knowledge Discovery and Data Mining
  • Knowledge Visualization
  • Social Networks
  • Medical Applications and Bioinformatics
  • Mobile Applications and Collaborative Mobile Spaces
  • New Approaches to Database and Knowledge Management Systems
  • Medical Applications and Bioinformatics
  • Recommendation Systems
  • Spatial Data Analysis
  • Web Technologies
PAPER SUBMISSIONS Authors are invited to submit original unpublished papers that demonstrate current research on one of the session topics of interest. Papers should be prepared in PDF using the Springer LNCS/LNAI style (8-15 pages). All submitted papers will be reviewed by the members of the ISMIS PC. Industry session papers should be submitted in PDF form via ISMIS 2020 Online Submission System. PUBLICATIONS OF PAPERS Accepted papers will be published in the edited post-conference book published in the Springer Series Studies in Computational Intelligence [https://www.springer.com/series/7092] The template for the camera-ready version can be found here. IMPORTANT DATES - Paper submission due: April 30, 2020 - Notification of review results: May 25, 2020 - Camera ready papers due: July 1, 2020 - Author registration/payment deadline: July, 15th, 2020 - Non-author registration/payment deadline: Aug. 15th, 2020 - Non-author late registration/payment deadline: Sep. 5th, 2020 - ISMIS’2020 conference date (online event): Sep, 23th-25th, 2020 INDUSTRY SESSION CO-CHAIRS - Martin Stettinger, Graz University of Technology - Gerhard Leitner, University of Klagenfurt
