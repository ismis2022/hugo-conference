We invite you to submit papers to the ISMIS’2022 Industry Session. The session is part of the ISMIS 2022 conference, which will be organized as an online event on Oct 3rd – 5th, 2022.


SCOPE AND OBJECTIVES
The session will be devoted to the applications of various intelligent methods, algorithms, and tools related to Artificial Intelligence in industry. The aim of this session is to create a forum for exchange of ideas between the leading researchers and industry practitioners. Submissions should address practical problems and/or business applications and should be innovative. Architectures, tools, and practical implementations related to the topics described below are also of interest to this session.

TOPICS OF INTEREST
Topics of interest include (but are not limited to) the following:
Applications of Intelligent Information Systems
Big Data
Cloud Computing
Context-aware Computing
Exploratory Data Analysis
Knowledge Discovery and Data Mining
Knowledge Visualization
Social Networks
Medical Applications and Bioinformatics
Mobile Applications and Collaborative Mobile Space
New Approaches to Database and Knowledge Management Systems
Medical Applications and Bioinformatics
Recommendation Systems
Spatial Data Analysis
Web Technologies

PAPER SUBMISSIONS
Authors are invited to submit original unpublished papers that demonstrate current research on one of the session topics of interest. Papers should be prepared in PDF using the Springer LNCS/LNAI style (8-15 pages). All submitted papers will be reviewed by the members of the ISMIS PC. Industry session papers should be submitted in PDF form via ISMIS 2022 Online Submission System.

PUBLICATIONS OF PAPERS
Accepted papers will be published in the edited post-conference book published in the Springer Series Studies in Computational Intelligence [https://www.springer.com/series/7092]
The template for the camera-ready version can be found here.
